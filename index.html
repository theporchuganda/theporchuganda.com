THE PORCH UGANDA

The porch uganda is a community based organisation started by a group of orphans who grew up on the mercy of God under a church based foundation to help the majority of people suffering in uganda and all its communities with different provisions afforded and solicited.

DIVISIONS OF OPERATION UNDER THE PORCH UGANDA

The Porch Uganda is divided into several sections for effectiveness of different operations with highly determined teams.

CHILDREN's SECT

Children are the future generation and a major concern for future fruitful communities thats why we designated a full section to handle several projects related to children life improvement . The different projects under this sect are; Helping homeless kids on streets get a place to live,helping orphans get a bright future,helping children being denied education and other basic needs and probably the major one fighting for children's rights.

WOMEN's FORUM

We are in a generation where every gender matters and the major cause for the creation of this division was to improve the lives of our dear mothers and sisters through the following implementation; we created women skilling programmes,fight for women rights in association with several women boards like FIDA uganda,communal teachings in fight against violence inflicted on women plus early girl child marriages.

YOUTH FOUNDATION

We aim at building a strong foundation for the youth in our communities from jobless and unskilled to great job creators under several skills. we are currently running several programmes for the youths including; computer training,craft making,agricultural skills and talent promotion.
